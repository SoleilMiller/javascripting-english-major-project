Soleil's Website on "Emperors Children" by Claire Messud

This website explores the locations in Emperors Children studies the historal, social and cultural relevance of the locations in New York City.

New York City is a pioneering city thats history spans back to the 16th century. The first European to discover, what we now know as, New York City was Giovanni Da Verrazzano, who was an Italian captain. The next group to discover the Area were the Dutch lead by Henry Hudson. The Dutch then started a trading port in lower Manhattan. It then became a Dutch Colony called New Amsterdam. After many attacks on the Dutch, the British finally captured the city and renamed it to what we now know as New York.Though New York has gone through many phases and financial shifts, the ecconomy is New York is now thriving. With the current real estate prices and cost of living, it is predicted that in the near future Manhattan will become an island for only the rich. This takes us to Emperor's Children, a book about the rich and want to be rich in the artistic field in New York City in the early 2000's.
